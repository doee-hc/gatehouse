About Gatehouse

Gatehouse is a self-improving multi-agent team plugin for OpenCode — role-based collaboration, Mission lifecycle management, and a visual Portal pixel office. Retrospectives and skill distillation feed back into future missions as the team evolves with your project.

The project is in early development; features may change or be incomplete. Use at your own risk.

GitHub repository github.com/doee-hc/gatehouse
